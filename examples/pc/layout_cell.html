Layout布局

协助进行页面级整体布局。

Cell布局


new mwt.CellLayout({
    render     : 'cell-div',
    rowCells   : 2,  //每行个数
    cellHeight : 60, //格子高度
    cellSpacing: 5,  //格子间隔
    items : [
        {id:'cell1',style:'background:#acd2f1',html:'#acd2f1'},
        {id:'cell2',style:'background:#aeedf5',html:'#aeedf5'},
        {id:'cell3',style:'background:#fbbdb9',html:'#fbbdb9'},
        {id:'cell4',style:'background:#ffdba5',html:'#ffdba5'},
        {id:'cell5',style:'background:#d9f9b4',html:'#d9f9b4'},
        {id:'cell6',style:'background:#b0e2de',html:'#b0e2de'}
    ]
}).init();
